Publications

  • 2024
    Puma: Fully Decentralized Uncertainty-Aware Multiagent Trajectory Planner with Real-Time Image Segmentation-Based Frame Alignment
    IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA)
    Co-authored with Kota Kondo, Claudius Tewari, Mason B. Peterson, Jouko Kinnari, Andrea Tagliabue, and Jonathan P. How, this paper presents Puma, a fully decentralized multiagent trajectory planner with real-time image segmentation, selected for ICRA 2024.
  • 2024
    SOS-Match: Segmentation for Open-Set Robust Correspondence Search and Robot Localization in Unstructured Environments
    IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS)
    Co-authored with Jouko Kinnari, Parker Lusk, Kota Konda, and Jonathan How, this paper introduces SOS-Match, a novel approach to robot localization in unstructured environments, selected for the 2024 IEEE/RSJ IROS.
  • 2024
    Outdoor Global Localization via Robust Registration of 3D Open-Set Segments
    arXiv preprint
    Co-authored with Mason Peterson, Yi Xuan Jia, Yulun Tian, and Jonathan How, this paper presents a method for outdoor global localization using robust registration of 3D open-set segments.
  • 2023
    Protecting Satellites in Low Earth Orbit: An Overview of Hazards and Policy Solutions
    MIT Science Policy Review
    Co-authored with Danisl Ammons, Kristen Green, Laman Jalil, Joe Kusters, and Kerri Cahoy, this article provides an overview of hazards facing satellites in low Earth orbit and policy solutions to protect them.
  • 2023
    Global Localization in Unstructured Environments Using Semantic Object Maps Built from Various Viewpoints
    IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS)
    Co-authored with Jacqueline Ankenbauer, Parker C. Lusk, and Jonathan P. How, this paper discusses global localization in unstructured environments using semantic object maps, selected for IROS 2023.
  • 2022
    Introducing Experimental Design to Promote Active Learning
    ASEE Annual Conference & Exposition
    Co-authored with Yevgeniy Yesilevskiy, Jessica Oehrlein, Melissa Wright, and Michael Tarnow, this paper discusses introducing experimental design into engineering education to promote active learning, presented at the 2022 ASEE Annual Conference.
  • 2020
    Real-Time Estimation of Electron Dynamics in Hall Effect Thrusters Using an Extended Kalman Filter
    AIAA Propulsion and Energy Forum
    Co-authored with Christine Greve, Manoranjan Majji, and Kentaro Hara, this paper explores real-time estimation of electron dynamics in Hall effect thrusters using an extended Kalman filter, presented at the AIAA Propulsion and Energy 2020 Forum.
